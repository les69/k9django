What is K9?

K9 is a tool for you to control your children from preventing pedophile or online bulling. We allow you to take a look to what your children does or search while they are surfing on the internet without you. The list below shows you what we're trying to detect in your children messages.


  • Pedophiles
  • Online bulling
  • Pornography dependency
  • Violence search

What is a badword?

A badword is a word that usually indicates a bad behaviour. Badwords can be harmless but they're used as indicators of a particular behaviour, for example the word Porn used many times can suggest a frequent search of porn web related items. We use a list of badwords to detect the behvaiours shown previously. Some badwords are


  • Porn
  • Nigger
  • Kill/Die
  • Hurt

What is a Positivity?

Positivity is only a number. Numbers can tell us great things, only if we know what they mean.
Positivity is a number that goes from -1 to 1, where 1 is the maximum level of positivity and -1 indicates negativity, 0 is for a neutral situation. This value is given by the positive weight given by words, for example words like beautiful or amazing has a really high positivity meanwhile hate or awful are very negative. The final value of a sentence is given by the average of every single word values.
If the level of positivity is decreasing in time, it's better to take actions and analyze what's happening.


And subjectivity?

Subjectivity is a value that behaves similarly to positivity, but rather than showing the positivity of a sentence it shows how it is personal or impersonal.


What does the chart shows?

The chart shows positivity and subjectivity in the time. Subjectivity gives you very little information while positivity shows in time the mood of your children. A descending chart means usually means that your children is having a problem and it's time to take serious actions.
